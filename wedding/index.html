Travel

Travel

Some recommendations for booking flights and accommodations.

Hotels

Sapphire Beach Resort
6720 Estate Smith Bay
St Thomas, USVI 00802

We recommend that you stay in the vicinity of Sapphire Beach Resort. The resort itself has timeshare condos that residents rent out on VRBO and AirBnB. We will be staying at Sapphire Beach Resort for our wedding. These units appear to be nicer than many of the hotels and less expensive. Sapphire Beach is where we will be hosting our Welcome Party and will also be the pickup location for the Wedding and Catamaran.
If you prefer a hotel over Airbnb/VRBO, the following options are also nearby: Secret Harbor Resort, Point Pleasant Resort , & Elysian Beach Resort.

The airport is located on the west side of the island, but we will be staying on the east side. We highly recommend you book a rental car. Taxis on the island are not as common and Uber/Lyft has not quite made it to paradise yet. USVI is still in the US so you do NOT need anything but your driver's license to rent a car. We recommend you do a quick search on KAYAK for the best available price.

Most of the island is a national park and it has some of the prettiest beaches in all of the Virgin Islands. Take your rental car on a quick 20 minute ferry ride out of Red Hook to get there. The ferry schedule is available online.
