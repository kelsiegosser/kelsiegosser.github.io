Answer

We love your kids, but we really want our wedding to be your day off! We have a fun, boozy day planned for you.

Answer

Yes, we will have transportation from Sapphire Beach Hotel to the wedding and catamaran. For more information about Sapphire Beach Hotel, check out Travel above.

Question

Can I bring a date?

Answer

Yes, please feel free to bring a guest. We want you to make the most out of this vacation!

Question

What is the dress code?

Answer

Beachwear! We will be getting married on the beach during the day so please feel free to wear shorts and a nice T-shirt. Shoes are welcome but unnecessary.

As for the Catamaran, please bring a swimsuit if you would like to swim and snorkel!
